Why We're Moving from Zoom to Google Meet

We are migrating our video conferencing platform from Zoom to Google Meet. This change allows us to take advantage of our investment in Google Workspace for Education Plus.

Benefits of Google Meet

  • Integrated experience: Google Meet works seamlessly with Gmail, Calendar, Docs, and Classroom, simplifying scheduling and collaboration.
  • Enhanced security: Meet is secured by Google infrastructure and offers built-in protections like encryption and meeting controls.
  • Higher participant limits: With Education Plus, meetings can host up to 500 participants and live streams can reach up to 100,000 viewers.
  • Recording & attendance reports: Automatically save meetings to Google Drive and track attendance with ease.
  • Cost efficiency: Consolidating on Google Meet eliminates our separate Zoom licensing fees.

Timeline

We plan to complete the transition by the end of the semester. In the meantime, both Zoom and Google Meet will be available while we migrate recurring meetings.

If you have questions, please reach out to the IT team.
